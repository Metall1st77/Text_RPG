







Command list:
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++continue: Continues a game after pause or loading the last saving.

Syntax: <continue>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------start: Starts a new game.

Syntax: <start>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------save: Saves current game session.

Syntax: <save>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------load: Loads a game from your saves.

Syntax: <load>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------show: Shows the statistics of anything you want to know.

Syntax: <show> <stats>/<log>/<saves>

stats: statistics of your character
log: statistics of the ingame data
saves: names of files you can load.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------attack: Calling your character to attack the nearest enemy.

Syntax: <attack> <dir=>

dir: direction (('left', 'right', 'up', 'down')).
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------defend: Calling your character to defend from the enemy.

Syntax: <defend>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------retire: Calling your character to retire from the battle. The enemy won't be defeated.

Syntax: <retire>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------payoff: Calling your character to pay off from your enemy. The enemy will be considered as defeated.

Syntax: <payoff> <cost=>

cost: amount of money you are going to pay.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------go: Calling your character to move to the specified place.

Syntax: <go> <dir=> <steps=>

dir: direction ('left', 'right', 'up', 'down').
steps: the quantity of steps you want your character to do (must be an integer number)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------move: The same as <go>.

Syntax: <move> <dir=> <steps=>

dir: direction ('left', 'right', 'up', 'down').
steps: the quantity of steps you want your character to do (must be an integer number)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------buy: Allows you to buy an item from the list of available.

Syntax: <buy> <item=>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------sell: Allows you to sell an item from your inventory.

Syntax: <sell> <item=>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------use: Allow you to use an item from your inventory.

Syntax: <use> <item=>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------equip: Equips an item from your inventory (only if the slot is empty).

Syntax: <equip> <item=>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------unequip: Unequips an item and puts it to your inventory.

Syntax: <unequip> <item=>
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
